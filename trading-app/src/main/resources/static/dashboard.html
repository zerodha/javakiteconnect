Getting Started

Welcome to Kite Connect Trading App! This MVP application provides you with the following features:

  • Trade: Place market and limit orders
  • Orders: View and manage your active orders
  • Holdings: View your long-term stock holdings
  • Positions: Monitor your intraday positions
  • Market Watch: Watch real-time quotes for your favorite stocks
  • Charts: View historical price data and charts
  • Margin Calculator: Calculate margin requirements before placing orders

Note: This is an MVP version. Additional features and improvements will be added in future phases.
